ABOUT ME

My name is Kevin Shao, and I am a 1st-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the University of Washington (UW), Seattle. I recently graduated with a master's degree from UW ECE.

Prior to that, I received my bachelor's degree with Magna Cum Laude honors in ECE from The Ohio State University (OSU), Columbus. I was also a member of the OSU Humanitarian Engineering Scholar Program.

Outside of study, I love to travel to see unqiue culture and history from different countries. Sketching and painting are also some great ways to make me relaxed.
